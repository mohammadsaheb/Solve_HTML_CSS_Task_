what is the indroduction of math ?

Math is the science that deals with the logic of shape, quantity and arrangement. Math is all around us, in everything we do. It is the building block for everything in our daily lives, including mobile devices, architecture (ancient and modern) , art, money, engineering, and even sports.

Math can help us to shop wisely, buy the right insurance, remodel a home within a budget, understand population growth, or even bet on the horse with the best chance

Mathnis a powerful tool for global understanding and communication. Using it, students can make sense of the world and solve complex and real problems.
